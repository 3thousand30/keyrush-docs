# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 19, 2026**

Key Rush is developed by [3thousand30](https://3thousand30.com/).

---

## What the app collects

Key Rush does not collect, store, or send your typing runs, settings, player names, or local leaderboard data to 3thousand30 servers.

- No account required
- No telemetry sent to us
- No analytics sent to us
- No crash reporting sent to us

---

## Data stored on your device

Key Rush stores local game data on your device so the app can remember your setup.

- Player names and local leaderboard stats
- Settings such as volume, motion, contrast, and font size
- Optional share images saved to `Downloads/KeyRush/`

This information stays on your device unless you choose to share a result or contact us.

---

## Share actions

When you choose a share action, Key Rush can:

- Generate a local PNG of your result card
- Save that PNG to `Downloads/KeyRush/`
- Copy share text or the PNG to your clipboard when supported
- Open external social or browser compose pages you explicitly choose

Those external sites are controlled by their own privacy policies.

---

## Third-party services you may open

Key Rush may open external sites you choose, such as:

- Microsoft Store
- X
- Threads
- Facebook
- LinkedIn
- Reddit
- WhatsApp
- 3thousand30 website pages

If you use those destinations, their privacy policies apply to that interaction.
