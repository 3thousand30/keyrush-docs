# Key Rush

**Key Rush** is a local-first desktop typing game by [3thousand30](https://3thousand30.com/). It mixes a hacker-terminal presentation with fast typing practice, short story missions, a falling-word pressure mode, and a calmer guided passage mode.

[Get it on Microsoft Store](https://apps.microsoft.com/detail/9MW7722B1026){: .btn .btn-blue }

---

## What Key Rush includes

- **Story mode** with 3 chapters and 9 levels
- **Endless mode** with falling promo words and a steady difficulty ramp
- **Zen mode** with bundled passages and random target words
- **Local players** and local leaderboards
- **Desktop result sharing** with saved PNG cards and external composers
- **Keyboard-first UI** with settings, players, help, and support pages
